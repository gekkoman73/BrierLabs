About

My name in Niko Marttinen and I am the founder of BrierLabs, a freelance data science and AI consultancy company helping companies to turn complex data and operational processes into better commercial decisions. M.Sc. in statistics, 20+ years across sports betting, trading, risk, and product management.

My core domain is in sports betting. I have led teams of odds compilers, been accountable for sportsbook's P&L, launched a sportsbook from scratch as Sports Betting Director. This puts me in a position to support operators and betting technology companies on pricing models, quant audits, data pipelines, and strategic consultancy.

At the same time, I also work well outside the sports betting domain. My experience in commodity trading, central bank statistics, product management, and operational IT gives me a broad toolkit for analytics projects where companies need someone who can understand the business problem, shape the data approach, and deliver practical, decision-oriented solutions.
